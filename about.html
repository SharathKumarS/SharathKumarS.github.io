HOWDY!

Welcome to my Website! I am Sharath Kumar S. A self-taught Graphic designer based in Bangalore, India. I’m constantly learning new things every day and love meeting people who are motivated.

I have learned a lot of the processes, concepts, and theories of UI and Graphic design by taking up online courses, reading blogs, and applying these to projects in my spare time. I do pencil sketching, and Digital Marketing too. I will not stop learning. I am very much a big-picture thinker and enjoy working on products end to end, from ideation all the way to design. I have showcased only recent works, for more please visit my Behance site.

Journey

In my childhood, I used to love watching animated movies, and colorful book covers and getting inspired by them. Finally, now I ended up pursuing my passion. When I’m away from my laptop, I will be busy taking care of my cat, playing PC games, reading some good books, and trying to get outside as much as possible, to enjoy the outdoors because we can learn a lot by observing nature.
