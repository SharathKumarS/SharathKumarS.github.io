About me

Graphic & UI Designer

Hello, I’m Sharath Kumar S!

Graphic & UI Designer

A self-taught graphic designer based in Bangalore, India, with a serious love for animals and an incurable curiosity for exploring new places and meeting interesting people—preferably ones who share snacks.

What I Do

Currently, I’m diving headfirst into the world of Lottie animations, illustrations, and UI design. Every day is a new learning adventure, and I’m always keen to discover new techniques and tools. My journey into design has been powered by online courses, countless blogs, and my own hands-on projects—with some caffeine and late nights in the mix.

A Little More About Me

When I’m not designing, you’ll find me indulging in a few other passions:

  • Reading books (Preferably, self-help ones).
  • Pencil sketching, because who doesn’t like a bit of old-school creativity?
  • PC gaming—Yes, I’m that person who says, “just one more level!”
  • Sometimes I just enjoy sitting in my balcony doing nothing...call it productive boredom.

    • I’m a big-picture thinker, always excited to work on projects from start to finish—taking ideas from vague concepts to polished designs.

    The Backstory

    As a kid, I was that kid—endlessly fascinated by animated movies and colorful book covers. Who knew those weekend marathons would eventually shape my career? And here I am today, doing what I love—designing cool stuff for a living!
